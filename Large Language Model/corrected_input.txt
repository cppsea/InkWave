Original:
I plege allegince to the falg of the Unieted States of Amercia, and two the repubilc four witch it stands, won nation, under God, invisble, with libirty and justise for all.

Corrected:
I pledge allegiance to the flag of the United States of America, and to the republic for which it stands, one nation, under God, indivisible, with liberty and justice for all.

Context:
The corrected text shows the Pledge of Allegiance to the flag of the United States of America. This pledge is a statement of loyalty and devotion to the country, emphasizing unity, faith in God, indivisibility, liberty, and justice for all individuals. It is commonly recited at public events and in schools as a show of patriotism.
